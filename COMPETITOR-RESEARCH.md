## 2. What People Actually PAY For (Ranked by Revenue)

### #1 — Animal Mandalas (Etsy's Biggest Paid Niche)
- 100 Animal Mandala pages: **$4–7** (343+ favorites per listing)
- 1,000 Animal Mandala pages: **$7–12** (Star Seller status)
- Lion Mandala 102 pages: **$5–9** (300 DPI, PDF)
- **Strategy: Offer animal mandala collections FREE → steal their traffic**

### #2 — Floral/Botanical Mandalas
- 30 Pretty Floral Mandalas: **$3–5** (269+ favorites)
- 25 Daisy Coloring Pages: **$2–4** (Star Seller)
- **Evergreen, year-round demand**

### #3 — Bold & Easy Stress Relief (Amazon's #1 Format)
- Dominates Amazon top 50 — Coco Wyo holds **20 of top 50** with this style
- Large simple designs, thick lines, not intricate
- Price: **$7.99–$9.99** per 40-page book
- **40% of all Amazon bestsellers use this exact framing**

### #4 — Cozy/Cute Animals (Coco Wyo brand — Amazon dominant)
- "Cozy Friends", "Girl Moments" style = cute animals + daily life characters
- Sells **5,000–15,000+ reviews** per title at $7.99
- **Almost no FREE website covers this aesthetic**

### #5 — Fantasy (Jade Summer owns Amazon)
- Fairies, Mermaids, Unicorns, Dragons
- 100,000+ five-star reviews across Jade Summer catalog
- **$7.99 per title, 10,000+ reviews each**

### #6 — Holiday/Seasonal Bundles
- Christmas is #1 single holiday
- Teachers buy **full-year bundles** on TPT for $8–20
- Color by Number math integration = TPT's #1 paid format

### #7 — Color by Number / Math Integration (TPT)
- **TPT's biggest coloring format** — tied to school curriculum
- Multiplication, addition, subtraction themed coloring
- Year-long seasonal bundle: **$9.50–$20**

## 8. Monetization Model (Based on Competitor Research)

**What works:** Free content + ads + optional Patreon/membership

| Stream | Model | Revenue |
|---|---|---|
| Google AdSense → Ezoic | Display ads on all free pages | $4–12 RPM |
| Patreon | $5/month for premium bundles (like coloringbliss.com) | Recurring |
| Amazon KDP | Publish top designs as physical books at $7.99 | $2–4 per sale |
| Etsy | Sell curated bundles (animal mandalas, floral) at $4–8 | Passive |
| Amazon Affiliate | Link to coloring supplies, pencils, markers | 3–8% commission |
